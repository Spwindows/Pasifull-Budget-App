Income & Pay Cycle

If you enter your real bank balance, the app will use that as the live amount available now. Otherwise it uses the carried balance + this week's income.
Change income each week if needed, then hit Start New Week on payday to archive the week, carry the remaining balance forward, clear current spends, and roll the pay dates forward.
